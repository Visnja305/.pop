Our Heritage

Roasting with Intention

Founded in Seattle's Capitol Hill in 2019, Elysian Roastery began with a simple belief: coffee roasting is both a rigorous science and a tactile art. We roast on a vintage, modified 15kg San Franciscan drum roaster, profiling each single-origin lot to bring out terroir-specific sweetness and clean acidity.

We build long-term relationships with growers in Colombia, Ethiopia, and Sumatra, paying far above fair-trade minimums to support regenerative agriculture and forest conservation. Every cup represents a transparent connection between grower, roaster, and brewer.
